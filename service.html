FAQ

Where's the best place for Dental surgery?
The best place for dental surgery depends on your location, but in Chennai, many patients recommend clinics like Dr. Smilez or Apollo White Dental for their technology and expertise.
Can a tooth infection affect the eyes?
Yes, in rare cases, an upper tooth infection can spread to the sinuses and affect areas near the eyes, potentially leading to swelling or even vision issues. Always treat infections early.
Can dental infections cause other problems?
Absolutely. Untreated dental infections can lead to abscesses, jawbone damage, or spread to other parts of the body, potentially causing serious systemic health issues.
Are clear aligners as good as Invisalign?
Clear aligners from other brands can be effective, but Invisalign is known for its advanced technology and results. However, quality varies, so always consult a certified orthodontist.
What is the best dental clinic in Chennai?
Top-rated dental clinics in Chennai include Dr. Smilez Dental Center, Apollo White Dental, and The Dental Studio. The best clinic for you depends on your specific needs and location.
