FAQ

Where's the best place for Dental surgery?
Smile Life Dental Clinic offers advanced dental surgeries, including implants and wisdom tooth removal, performed by skilled specialists using modern, minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery.
Can a tooth infection affect the eyes?
Yes, in rare cases, a severe tooth infection can spread to nearby areas, including the sinuses and tissues around the eyes, potentially causing swelling, pain, or vision issues if left untreated.
Can dental infections cause other problems?
Absolutely. Untreated dental infections can lead to abscesses, jawbone damage, or spread to other parts of the body, potentially causing serious systemic health issues.
Are clear aligners as good as Invisalign?
Clear aligners from other brands can be effective, but Invisalign is known for its advanced technology and results. However, quality varies, so always consult a certified orthodontist.
