Hygiene & Safety

Clean, Calm, and Completely Safe

Your safety is our top priority. That’s why every clinic at Smile Life Dental is equipped with hospital-level hygiene and sterilization systems. From the dental chair to every instrument we use, cleanliness is never compromised. Our protocols include Class B autoclaves, sealed sterilization pouches, one-time-use disposables, and surface disinfectants that exceed industry standards.
